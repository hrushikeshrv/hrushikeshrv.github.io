ABOUT US

The Dr. Shanta Vaidya Foundation works with children who face difficulty in reading, writing, or math, in spite of having average to above average intelligence and equal opportunities to learn. This condition is known as a Specific Learning Disability (SLD) or Dyslexia.

We are based in Pune, Maharashtra, India and at present works in the city of Pune and surrounding districts in Maharashtra.

OUR VISION

To nurture potential through creation of opportunities for enrichment of the lives of children with Specific Learning Difficulties.

OUR MISSION

Ability enhancement of children with Specific Learning Difficulties through emotional, intellectual, and physical development by involvement of all stakeholders.
