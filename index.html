DR. SHANTA VAIDYA MEMORIAL FOUNDATION

The Dr. Shanta Vaidya Foundation works with children who experience difficulty in reading, writing, and math inspite of having average to above average intelligence. This condition is popularly known as Specific Learning Disability (SLD), or Dyslexia.

Does My Child Have Dyslexia?

Does your child show these signs?

  • Reads slowly. Misses out words or lines while reading.
  • Shows poor comprehension.
  • Writes in a messy or poor handwriting.
  • Cannot complete school notebooks and exam papers
  • Hardly remembers multiplication tables or poems.
  • Finds math sums difficult.
  • Understands the concepts taught in school but still gets poor marks in examinations.
  • Cannot sit in one spot easily and gets distracted easily.

Dyslexia affects 1 out of 10 people in India. Please check our resources page to find helpful material including the International Dyslexia Foundation's self-assessment tool.
